About Fitzpatrick Sales

Fitzpatrick Sales will provide the Highest Quality Sales, Marketing, Training and Communication available to our Customers to provide for Mutual Profitable Growth for the Manufacturers we represent, The Customers we serve and Our Company.

We will do this with Honesty, Integrity and without compromising our values.

Why should you and your customers trust Fitzpatrick Sales?

With 150 years combined experience, a full time quotations department, inside sales, and outside sales team, Fitzpatrick Sales can meet any of the needs you may have. Do you need training? Technical Support? Order Status? The Team at Fitzpatrick Sales is always available to help!
